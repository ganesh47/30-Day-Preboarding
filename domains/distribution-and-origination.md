# Distribution and Origination

## What this domain is
How deal flow is sourced, screened, and converted into disbursals with quality and speed controls.

## Why it matters in real lending businesses
Origination quality sets the base rate of future portfolio risk.

## What I need to be fluent in
- Channel mix and incentive alignment
- Funnel metrics from lead to sanctioned to disbursed
- Fraud and misrepresentation controls at sourcing stage

## How decisions are actually made
Commercial urgency is balanced against quality gates, documentation readiness, and concentration limits.

## Where things break in reality
- Misaligned sales incentives
- Weak first-mile verification
- Pipeline opacity causing adverse selection

## Product/tech leverage points
- Channel scorecards linked to downstream performance
- Workflow controls for documentation and deviations
- Concentration alerts by geography/sector/borrower group

## Signals that I understand this well
- I can trace future delinquency back to origination choices.
- I can redesign funnel stages to reduce bad approvals.
- I can identify incentive changes that improve quality.
