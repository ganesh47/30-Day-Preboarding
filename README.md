# Lending Deep Dive Operating System

## Why this repository exists
I am using this repository as a disciplined, public workspace to build operator-level judgment in corporate and mid-market lending. The goal is not to memorize terminology. The goal is to make better decisions about risk, structure, monitoring, and recovery.

## What I aim to understand
- How lending businesses create risk-adjusted returns across a full loan lifecycle.
- How losses emerge in practice: weak underwriting, poor structure, delayed signals, or ineffective collections.
- How product and technology can improve decision quality, control quality, and speed without weakening risk discipline.
- How credit committees evaluate trade-offs when data is incomplete and outcomes are uncertain.

## Learning philosophy
- **Decision over definition:** every topic must translate into an approval, pricing, structuring, or monitoring choice.
- **Lifecycle thinking:** disbursement is an input; repayment and recovery determine outcomes.
- **Operator mindset:** focus on incentives, controls, exception handling, and execution reality.
- **Write to think:** every day ends with a written artifact, reflection, and next action.

## Three layers of focus
1. **Credit & risk fundamentals**  
   Borrower quality, cash flow durability, leverage tolerance, and downside protection.
2. **Deal structuring & capital flows**  
   Covenants, security packages, repayment design, waterfall mechanics, and investor constraints.
3. **Operating system of lending (origination → recovery)**  
   Pipeline quality, credit decisioning, post-disbursal monitoring, collections execution, and resolution strategy.

## Repository structure
- `/domains/` — core business domains that shape lending outcomes.
- `/corporate-credit/` — practical corporate credit analysis and committee mechanics.
- `/learning-path/` — 4-week progression with weekly objectives and outputs.
- `/tracker/` — daily logs, weekly reviews, and executive briefs.
- `/github-ops/` — milestones, labels, and issue execution model.
- `/play/` — quizzes, mini-cases, flashcards, and scoring.
- `/leadership-view/` — strategic narrative and leadership discussion topics.
- `/resources/` — reading list and reference links.
- `/notes/` — ad hoc working notes.
- `/glossary/` — compact operating glossary.
- `/issues/` — detailed daily issue prompts and deliverables.

## Progress tracking via GitHub Issues
- Work is executed through issue-driven daily sprints.
- Each issue has objective, tasks, mini deliverable, reflection, and a completion checklist.
- Weekly milestones force closure and synthesis rather than endless reading.

## What success looks like
- I can explain why a deal should be approved, declined, or restructured using first-principles reasoning.
- I can identify where expected losses can emerge before delinquency is visible.
- I can connect business model choices to portfolio behavior, unit economics, and recovery outcomes.
- I can produce concise executive summaries that are actionable for risk, business, and product stakeholders.
