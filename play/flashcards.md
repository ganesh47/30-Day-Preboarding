# Flashcards

- **Q:** What matters more than disbursal volume?  
  **A:** Cash collected net of losses and operating cost.

- **Q:** What is a practical covenant test?  
  **A:** A threshold that triggers action before repayment capacity collapses.

- **Q:** What is the first question in credit committee?  
  **A:** What is the downside path and can we survive it?
