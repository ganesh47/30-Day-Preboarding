# Collections and Recovery

## What this domain is
The system of actions that preserves repayment behavior, resolves delinquency, and maximizes recoveries after stress.

## Why it matters in real lending businesses
Profitability depends on cash actually collected. Weak collections destroys returns even with strong origination.

## What I need to be fluent in
- Delinquency buckets and cure behavior
- Collections strategy by borrower profile and stress type
- Legal vs negotiated resolution trade-offs

## How decisions are actually made
Collections strategy is triaged by probability of cure, collateral enforceability, and time-to-cash.

## Where things break in reality
- Late intervention after stress becomes entrenched
- Poor segmentation and one-size-fits-all treatment
- Incomplete borrower engagement history

## Product/tech leverage points
- Promise-to-pay tracking and contact strategy optimization
- Delinquency trajectory models for early intervention
- Recovery analytics by channel and legal stage

## Signals that I understand this well
- I can prioritize accounts by expected incremental recovery.
- I can explain when legal escalation is value-destructive.
- I can design KPIs beyond simple collection rate.
