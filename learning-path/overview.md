# 4-Week Learning Path Overview

## Objective
Build operational judgment across underwriting, structuring, monitoring, and recovery with daily written outputs.

## Weekly arc
- Week 1: Lending machine fundamentals and repayment logic
- Week 2: Corporate credit analysis and committee-quality writing
- Week 3: Structure, covenants, monitoring, and stress response
- Week 4: Resolution strategy, synthesis, and leadership-grade articulation

## Cadence
- Daily: one issue, one artifact, one reflection
- Weekly: synthesis brief + operating adjustments
