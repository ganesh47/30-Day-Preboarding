# Structured Finance

## What this domain is
Design of cash-flow structures that allocate risk and return across instruments, investors, and time.

## Why it matters in real lending businesses
Structure determines capital efficiency, funding stability, and loss allocation during stress.

## What I need to be fluent in
- Waterfalls, triggers, reserve accounts, and credit enhancement
- Pool stratification and collateral performance metrics
- Investor covenant requirements and reporting expectations

## How decisions are actually made
Structuring is an optimization problem: risk transfer, cost of funds, operational feasibility, and regulatory constraints are negotiated simultaneously.

## Where things break in reality
- Model assumptions diverge from servicing performance
- Trigger design fails to activate early enough
- Data quality gaps undermine investor confidence

## Product/tech leverage points
- Waterfall simulation tooling
- Data lineage controls for pool reporting
- Trigger monitoring dashboards with breach forecasting

## Signals that I understand this well
- I can map who absorbs first loss under multiple scenarios.
- I can explain why a structure is resilient or fragile.
- I can identify which covenants matter most for investors.
