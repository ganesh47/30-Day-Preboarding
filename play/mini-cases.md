# Mini Cases

## Case 1: Working Capital Trap
- Situation: Revenue growth with rising debtor days and short-term debt dependence.
- Task: recommend exposure decision and covenant package.

## Case 2: Sector Shock
- Situation: Regulatory change impacts cash conversion cycle.
- Task: redesign stress assumptions and monitoring frequency.

## Case 3: Delinquency Creep
- Situation: no formal default, but payment behavior weakens over 90 days.
- Task: choose intervention strategy and escalation trigger.
