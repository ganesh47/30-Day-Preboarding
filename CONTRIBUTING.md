# Contributing

## Principles
- Keep contributions practical and decision-focused.
- Avoid generic summaries; prioritize how decisions are made and where they fail.
- Use clear headings, checklists, and concise language.

## Workflow
1. Open or pick an issue.
2. Add/update one artifact tied to that issue.
3. Add reflection and unresolved questions.
4. Link deliverable in issue comments.

## Quality bar
- Every new note should include: decision context, risk lens, and execution implications.
- Prefer examples and edge cases over definitions.
- If uncertain, list assumptions explicitly.
