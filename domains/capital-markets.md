# Capital Markets

## What this domain is
The funding ecosystem through which lenders raise debt/equity, manage liquidity, and align tenor with assets.

## Why it matters in real lending businesses
Cost and reliability of capital determine pricing power, growth pace, and resilience during volatility.

## What I need to be fluent in
- Liability mix, tenor matching, and refinancing risk
- Investor expectations on portfolio quality and transparency
- Spread dynamics and market access constraints

## How decisions are actually made
Treasury, risk, and business teams balance growth opportunities against liquidity buffers, pricing discipline, and market windows.

## Where things break in reality
- Asset-liability mismatch
- Overdependence on one funding channel
- Delayed response to widening spreads

## Product/tech leverage points
- ALM stress dashboards and refinancing calendars
- Early warning signals for funding concentration risk
- Investor reporting packs with drill-down transparency

## Signals that I understand this well
- I can quantify refinancing risk under stressed markets.
- I can explain pricing decisions using funding constraints.
- I can identify when growth should be deliberately slowed.
