# Issue 003 — Read a Balance Sheet

## Objective
Translate balance sheet items into credit risk implications.

## Tasks
- Analyze liquidity, leverage, and working capital quality.
- Identify hidden stress in receivables, inventory, or short-term borrowings.
- Link findings to repayment risk over next 12 months.

## Mini deliverable
Credit-focused balance sheet note (max 500 words).

## Reflection question
Which balance sheet change would force immediate re-underwriting?

## Optional stretch goal
Draft three monitoring triggers from this analysis.

## Checklist
- [ ] Liquidity assessment completed
- [ ] Leverage analysis completed
- [ ] Risk translation completed
- [ ] Reflection logged
